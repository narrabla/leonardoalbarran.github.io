City of Helsinki — Operations Platform

Co-Founder & Product Manager · Public sector · Workflow digitization

Snapshot

  • Role: Co-Founder & Product Manager
  • Timeframe: 2016–2021
  • Client: City of Helsinki
  • Outcome: 20% cost reduction in seasonal street-cleaning over 3 years
  • Tech: Node.js · MongoDB · AWS · mobile + web interfaces

The context

The City of Helsinki needed a digital solution to optimize seasonal street-cleaning operations. The existing process relied heavily on manual coordination and limited real-time visibility, leading to inefficiencies and avoidable costs.

The challenge

  • Multiple stakeholders and user types (officials, operators, administrators).
  • Public sector delivery constraints: formal approvals, scope control, and clear documentation.
  • Real-world operational dependencies requiring reliability and ease of use.

My responsibilities

  • Stakeholder workshops and discovery
  • Requirements gathering and prioritization
  • Roadmap definition and delivery planning
  • UX direction and feedback loops
  • Coordination with engineering on architecture and implementation
  • Communication with public stakeholders throughout delivery

Key initiatives

1) Stakeholder-driven discovery

  • Mapped workflows and bottlenecks with city officials and field operators.
  • Translated operational needs into structured requirements and a prioritized roadmap.

2) Workflow digitization and operational visibility

  • Designed digital workflows to reduce manual coordination and improve decision-making.
  • Ensured usability for operators working under real-world constraints.

Outcome: contributed to 20% cost reduction over 3 years.

3) Delivery in a public-sector environment

  • Maintained scope discipline and clear milestone tracking.
  • Validated progress continuously with stakeholders to ensure adoption and impact.

Learnings

  • Clarity and stakeholder alignment drive adoption more than feature volume.
  • Operational systems need reliability and usability over novelty.
  • Measurable impact is the strongest success metric in public sector work.
