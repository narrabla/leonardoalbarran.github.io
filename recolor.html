Recolor (iOS & Android)

Product Owner · Subscription + Ads · Mobile app at scale

Aug 2024 – Present · Team ~15 · Jira/Confluence · RevenueCat

Snapshot

  • Role: Product Owner
  • Timeframe: Aug 2024 – Present
  • Team: ~15 (Engineering, Design, QA, Data, Marketing)
  • Business model: Subscription + Ads
  • Tools: Jira, Confluence, Sigma, AppsFlyer, Firebase, DeltaDNA, Google Analytics, Meta Business Manager, AppFollow, RevenueCat, Google Ads

Product context (scale)

  • 50M+ downloads (iOS + Android)
  • $52M lifetime revenue (subscriptions + ads)
  • Peak 3.7M MAU (strong Tier-1 audience)
  • Community: 70M published pictures · 400M+ images coloured · 1.6B likes
  • US Top 10 Entertainment (App Store) · Google Play Editor’s Choice · #1 Entertainment in 50+ countries

These metrics represent lifetime product performance over ~10 years and are included for product-scale context.

The challenge

  • Modernize the experience without disrupting an established user base.
  • Improve subscription conversion in a competitive market.
  • Maintain stability and quality across iOS and Android while shipping new features.
  • Balance feature delivery with platform/technical improvements.

PO lens: focus on outcomes, prioritization, subscription performance, and KPI-driven iteration.

Producer lens: focus on delivery cadence, quality bar, release planning, and cross-team coordination.

My responsibilities

  • Roadmap and backlog ownership
  • Feature definition and prioritization
  • Cross-functional execution and delivery planning
  • KPI monitoring and performance analysis
  • Collaboration with marketing and growth teams
  • Stakeholder communication and launch decisions

Key initiatives & impact

1) RevenueCat integration (subscription optimization)

Problem: Improve subscription performance and enable faster iteration on subscription strategy.

What I did:

  • Led the initiative end-to-end (scope, priorities, delivery plan).
  • Partnered with engineering and analytics to ensure correct implementation and tracking.
  • Coordinated rollout planning and post-release monitoring.

How I produced it:

  • Aligned cross-team dependencies and release plan.
  • Defined acceptance criteria and validation steps.
  • Managed rollout risk and post-release monitoring.

Result: Increased subscription conversion and sales by 30%.

2) iOS UI modernization (Liquid Glass UI migration)

Problem: Refresh the iOS experience to align with modern platform expectations and improve consistency.

  • Owned the roadmap initiative and delivery coordination.
  • Defined priorities and phased rollout approach.
  • Balanced UX improvements with delivery risk and platform stability.

Result: Successful UI modernization and improved visual consistency.

3) Roadmap execution & feature delivery at scale

  • Managed roadmap planning and backlog prioritization.
  • Aligned engineering, design, QA, data, and marketing on priorities and tradeoffs.
  • Used analytics + user feedback to guide decisions and release priorities.

Result: Delivered multiple improvements while maintaining stability.
